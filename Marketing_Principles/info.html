Our Proposition

“Climate change isn’t someone else’s problem—it’s ours to solve, right here, right now. Our actions, big or small, matter. By committing to five achievable climate- friendly changes over three months, each Wisconsinite can be part of the movement to preserve our state, our nation, and our planet. Together, we can turn the tide.”​


How to Earn the Tax Credit? Complete Tasks

1. Reduce Home Energy Use by 20% – Use LED bulbs, adjust the thermostat, unplug devices, and use energy-efficient appliances.​

2. Enroll in a Renewable Energy Program – switch to a renewable energy provider.​

3. Your Home – Add insulation, seal windows, and upgrade to energy-efficient appliances to reduce heating/cooling costs.​

4. Use Public Transit, Bike, or Carpool ​

5. Drive Less, Walk More – Commit to walking or biking for all trips under 2 miles.​

6. Compost Food Waste – Set up a composting system at home or participate in a community composting program.​

7. Reduce Single-Use Plastics – Use reusable shopping bags, water bottles, and containers instead of disposable plastic items.​

8.   Buy Local & Sustainable Products – Support local farmers and sustainable brands by purchasing food and goods produced with minimal environmental impact.​

9. Cut Back on Unnecessary Purchases – Track spending and avoid buying non-essential, high- carbon-footprint products.​

10. Volunteer for a Climate Group – Spend at least 10 hours over three months helping environmental organizations.​

11. Friends, Family, or Coworkers – Spread the word and encourage others to take action by signing them up for the program.​

12. Plant Trees or Support a Reforestation Project – Participate in local tree-planting efforts or donate to organizations that restore forests.​

Key Information

- You only need to complete 5 Tasks!.
- Record proof of completing the tasks to recieve the credit.
- Remember the world needs your help!
